Credit Card
Fraud Detection

The dataset comprises transactions made with European credit cards in September 2013, capturing activities over a two-day period. It includes a total of 284,807 transactions, of which 492 were identified as fraudulent. To analyze this data, exploratory data analysis techniques were employed alongside machine learning models, including RandomForest and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), to tackle the classification tasks.

Traffic Sign
Reconition (CNN)

The German Traffic Sign Benchmark is a multi-class, single-image classification challenge that was featured at the International Joint Conference on Neural Networks (IJCNN) in 2011. For this project, a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model was developed and trained to perform the classification tasks.
